About

Kyra Taormina - Founder & CEO

I am a Floral Design graduate from Switzerland with over 6 years of floral design experience. I combine Swiss inspired styles with American designs to create unique floral arrangements. I pride myself in being able to maintain top quality while also working within my client's budgetary limits. Having worked many weddings (and having recently been married myself), I understand how important floral arrangements are to weddings and events. I'm a detail-oriented person who strives to obtain the highest level of satisfaction from my clientele.
